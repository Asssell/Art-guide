Biography

Vincent Van Gogh was a Dutch post-impressionist painter, whose work had a far-reaching influence on 20th-century art. He is known for his emotional intensity, bold color choices, and innovative techniques, which helped shape modern art.

He is among the most famous and influential figures in the history of Western art, with iconic works such as *Starry Night*, *Sunflowers*, and *The Bedroom*.
