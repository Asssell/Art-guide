Biography

Michelangelo Buonarroti (1475–1564) was an Italian sculptor, painter, and architect, widely considered one of the greatest artists of all time. He is best known for his masterpieces such as the ceiling of the Sistine Chapel and the iconic statue of David. Michelangelo's works had a profound influence on the development of Western art.
