Biography

Rembrandt van Rijn (1606–1669) was a Dutch Golden Age painter known for his exceptional use of light and shadow, and his profound self-portraits. His works often reflect a deep emotional and psychological exploration of his subjects, making him one of the most influential artists in European art history.
