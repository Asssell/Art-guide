Biography

Leonardo da Vinci (1452–1519) was an Italian Renaissance artist and polymath, widely considered one of the greatest artists of all time. He is best known for his masterpieces like the Mona Lisa and The Last Supper. Da Vinci was also an inventor, scientist, and engineer, whose notebooks filled with sketches and designs are among the most important documents in the history of science.
