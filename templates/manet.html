Biography

Édouard Manet (1832–1883) was a French modernist artist, known for works like Olympia and Le Déjeuner sur l'herbe, challenging traditional artistic norms. His bold style and revolutionary approach were key to the development of Impressionism, even though he never fully embraced the movement.
