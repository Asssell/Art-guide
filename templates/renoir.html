Biography

Pierre-Auguste Renoir (1841–1919) was a French Impressionist artist, best known for his vivid and lively portraits, including works like Luncheon of the Boating Party. Renoir's works often capture the joy of life and the beauty of everyday moments, especially focusing on Parisian social scenes.
