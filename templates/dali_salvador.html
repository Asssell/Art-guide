Biography

Salvador Dali (1904–1989) was a Spanish surrealist artist known for his eccentric and imaginative works. He became famous for his surrealist paintings that explored dream-like scenes, often featuring melting clocks and distorted figures. Dali’s work was influenced by Freudian theory and his own vivid dreams.
